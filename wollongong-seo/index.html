How It Works

From keywords to conversations: why SEO and AEO are two sides of the same coin.

Plenty of agencies treat Answer Engine Optimisation as a completely separate discipline from Search Engine Optimisation. We think that's a mistake — AEO is the natural evolution of good SEO, not a different framework entirely.

Traditional SEO is about structuring your website, targeting the right keywords, and building authority so search engines can properly index your pages. AEO takes that same foundation and refines it for how modern search actually works today.

When Google shows an AI Overview at the top of a results page, or someone asks Gemini "who's the most reliable commercial electrician in Wollongong?" — those systems aren't guessing. They're pulling from technically sound, highly authoritative, schema-mapped websites.

If your foundational SEO is strong, you're already halfway to mastering AEO. Combine both, and your business shows up whether someone's scrolling Google or asking an AI assistant.

FAQs

Wollongong SEO + AEO — your questions answered.

What is the main difference between SEO and AEO?
Traditional SEO focuses on optimising your website to rank as high as possible in standard search engine results pages for specific keywords. AEO (Answer Engine Optimisation) focuses on structuring your information so AI systems like ChatGPT, Google Gemini, and Apple Intelligence can easily find, synthesise, and display your business as the direct answer to conversational questions.
Why do I need both for my Wollongong business?
Because the way people look for local services is fragmenting. While plenty of people still use standard Google search, a growing number ask conversational questions directly to voice assistants or AI apps. Running a combined strategy captures both demographics properly.
How long does it take to see measurable results?
Think of search performance like physical fitness — you wouldn't expect to hit the gym once and instantly transform. It takes consistent, structured effort over time. Technical fixes and Google Business Profile optimisation can yield early Maps movement within the first 60 to 90 days, while a comprehensive campaign typically needs 6 to 12 months of sustained work to hit peak velocity and deliver a compounding return.
Do you offer lock-in contracts?
No. We believe in earning your partnership every single month based on clear data, transparent communication, and measurable growth in your leads and revenue. If you're not happy, simply cancel with 30 days' written notice.
Can I bundle SEO with Google Ads?
Yes — in fact, that's one of our core strengths. Combining local SEO with targeted local Google Ads creates a dominant search footprint, capturing searchers who click on ads and those who click on organic Maps results.
Is local hosting important for Wollongong SEO?
Absolutely. Speed is a primary Google ranking factor. Our hosting infrastructure hosts your site locally in Australia, delivering lightning-fast load times for local searchers, which significantly boosts your organic rankings.
Do I really need blog articles for my local business?
Yes, but only if they're strategic. We look at local search volume and find out exactly what questions your customers in Wollongong are asking — such as "How much does a house extension cost in the Illawarra?" or "What are the council approval steps for a renovation in Shellharbour?" Answering these specific questions captures high-value traffic your competitors are completely ignoring.
What do I need to provide to get started?
We keep onboarding simple. Once we agree on a strategy, we'll just need temporary access to your website backend, Google Analytics, and Google Search Console. If you don't have analytics set up yet, our team will handle the full configuration for you.
How do you measure success and report on progress?
We focus on metrics that impact your bottom line — organic traffic growth, keyword rankings (both traditional and AI search citations), phone calls, and lead form submissions. You'll receive clear monthly reports and direct communication, so you always know what we're working on and how your investment is performing.
How much do SEO and AEO services cost?
Our packages are customised based on your industry's competitiveness, your geographic target area, and your growth goals. We offer flexible monthly retainers designed specifically for local service businesses and growing brands — with no hidden fees or long lock-in contracts.
Can you guarantee a #1 ranking on Google?
No agency can ethically guarantee a #1 spot because Google constantly updates its algorithms and no one owns the search engine. What we do guarantee is a data-driven, white-hat SEO strategy built on best practices that consistently drives higher search visibility, increased traffic, and genuine local leads to your business.
How do you optimize my website for AI search engines like ChatGPT and Gemini?
We structure your website's content so AI language models can easily read, understand, and quote your business. This involves implementing advanced schema markup (structured data), organising content in direct Q&A formats, building conversational keyword strategies, and ensuring your business entity is verified across key authoritative web directories.
Will AEO help my business appear in Google's AI Overviews?
Yes. Google's AI Overviews pull directly from sites that provide clear, authoritative, and structured answers to complex queries. By optimising your site for Answer Engine Optimisation (AEO), we increase the likelihood that Google's AI selects your business as a primary source in its generated search summaries.
Do you help with Google Business Profile and Google Maps rankings?
Absolutely. For local Wollongong businesses, local map rankings are essential. We fully optimise your Google Business Profile, manage local citations, build regional backlinks, and optimise your location pages to ensure you rank high in Google's Local 3-Pack.
Do you target customers outside Wollongong, like Shellharbour or Sydney?
Yes. Whether you want to capture leads across the Illawarra, the South Coast, or expand into Greater Sydney and nationwide, we craft tailored multi-location SEO and AEO strategies to reach your target geographic regions.
Can you work with my existing website, or do I need a new one?
In most cases, we work directly with your existing website (WordPress, Shopify, Webflow, Squarespace, etc.). If technical issues or slow speeds are holding your rankings back, we will advise you on the necessary on-site optimisations first to make sure you get results.
