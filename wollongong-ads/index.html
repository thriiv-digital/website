Google Ads vs Meta Ads

Which platform is right for your business?

The short answer: it depends on whether your customers are actively searching for you right now, or if they need to be introduced to what you offer.

Google Ads
Choose Google Ads if people already know they need your service — emergency repairs, professional services, local trades. They're typing specific keywords into Google, looking to call or book someone immediately.
emergency plumber near me quote today
Meta Ads
Choose Meta Ads if your service is visual, offers a clear before-and-after transformation, or requires educating the customer first. Ideal for generating new demand, building local brand awareness, and retargeting people who visited your site.

FAQs

Wollongong Google Ads & Meta Ads — your questions answered.

Should I start with Google Ads or Meta Ads?
It depends on your business. Google Ads is ideal if people are actively searching for your service right now (e.g. plumbers, lawyers, emergency repairs). Meta Ads works exceptionally well for visual services, generating demand, and retargeting people who visited your site. During our strategy call, we'll recommend the highest-ROI entry point for your budget.
What is the minimum budget needed for ad campaigns?
Your ad spend goes directly to the ad platforms. We typically recommend a starting ad budget of $30 to $50 per day to ensure your campaigns collect enough data to optimise quickly and drive consistent, daily lead volume.
Are Facebook and Instagram leads lower quality than Google search leads?
They can be if built lazily, as social ads interrupt a user's feed. However, we counter this entirely by using "higher intent" form layouts, context cards, and injecting strict pre-qualifying questions directly into the form to filter out accidental clicks.
Do I need to supply my own photos and videos for Meta Ads?
It helps, but you don't need a professional camera crew. Authenticity beats high production value every time — crisp smartphone video of your team on a local job site or clean before-and-after photos usually outperform polished corporate graphics. We'll tell you exactly what quick snaps to send us.
How quickly will I start getting leads?
Once your campaigns, landing pages, and lead forms go live, your ads appear instantly. Most of our clients start receiving direct phone calls and inquiries within the first week of launch.
How do you keep ads focused strictly on my service areas?
We apply geo-fencing and suburb-level targeting parameters, ensuring your budget is spent exclusively on users physically located within your chosen service boundary — for example, Helensburgh down to Kiama.
Do I own my ad accounts and landing pages, or do you lock me in?
You retain 100% ownership of your Google Ads, Meta Ads Manager accounts, and domain data. We build and manage everything transparently under your account so you maintain full access and data control — even if you ever decide to stop working with us.
How do I track where my leads and phone calls are coming from?
We set up end-to-end conversion tracking on your landing pages and lead forms, along with dynamic call tracking numbers. This means every time someone submits a form or calls your phone, you'll know exactly which keyword, ad, or platform brought them to you.
What happens if someone clicks my ad but doesn't fill out the form right away?
Most prospects don't buy on the very first touchpoint. We can build cross-platform retargeting funnels so that people who visit your Google landing page without booking will start seeing your brand, client testimonials, and special offers on Facebook and Instagram until they're ready to convert.
What is a "negative keyword", and why does it save me money on Google?
Negative keywords prevent your ads from showing up when people type in irrelevant terms (like "DIY", "jobs", "free", or competitor brand names). By constantly updating your negative keyword shield, we prevent wasted clicks so every dollar goes toward high-intent local buyers.
Do you require long-term lock-in contracts?
No. We operate on flexible monthly retainers because we believe our results should earn your business every month. We ask for a short initial period just to allow the ad algorithms to optimise, but after that, you are never trapped in a multi-year contract.
What do I need to prepare before we launch our ad campaigns?
We handle all the heavy lifting — writing ad copy, setting up targeting, and building your landing pages or lead forms. All we need from you is access to your ad accounts (or we can set them up with you), basic business details, and a few real photos/videos of your team at work.
