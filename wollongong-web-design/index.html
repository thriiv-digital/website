The Hard Truth

Your website could be quietly costing you the job.

We've audited more Illawarra websites than we can count, and the same problems come up over and over. If any of these sound familiar, your website isn't neutral — it's actively working against you.

The First Impression

It looks like it was built five years ago — because it probably was

A dated, template-heavy site doesn't just look tired, it actively undermines trust before anyone's even picked up the phone. Wollongong customers compare you to your competitors in seconds, and a site that looks stuck in the past tells them to keep scrolling.

The Invisible Website

It was never actually built to rank

Plenty of sites are built with no suburb references, no local schema, and no service-area structure — which gives Google no reason to show it for local searches. Your competitors show up in the map pack while you sit on page four, wondering why the phone isn't ringing.

The Slow Leak

It loads slowly on the phone, right where your customers are searching

The vast majority of local searches in the Illawarra happen on a mobile phone, often from someone who wants a job sorted fast. A site that takes more than a couple of seconds to load on 4G loses most visitors before the page even finishes rendering.

The Dead End

It gets visitors, but gives them nothing to actually do

Traffic is only half the job. If your site doesn't guide people clearly toward calling, quoting, or booking — with a confusing structure or a form buried three clicks deep — it's not working for your business no matter how many people land on it.

FAQs

Wollongong web design — your questions, answered.

How much does a new website cost?
It depends on the scope — a straightforward brochure-style site for a trade business is a very different job to a multi-service site for a growing professional practice. Rather than guess, we'd rather have a quick conversation about what you actually need and give you a clear, itemised quote with no hidden extras.
Do you offer fixed pricing, or is every quote different?
Every website is quoted individually because the scope varies so much. Rather than publish a generic price list that doesn't actually apply to your situation, we'd rather have a quick conversation and give you a clear, itemised quote with no hidden extras and no surprise invoices later.
How long does a full website development project take?
A premium, custom website build typically takes between 1-4 weeks from onboarding to launch. That timeline lets our local team carefully craft your content, build the design, and technically optimise every single page for speed and conversion performance.
I don't have a website at all — can you still help?
Completely fine — plenty of the businesses we work with are building their first website, not replacing one. If anything, it's simpler: there's no legacy content to untangle and no rankings to protect during a migration, just a clean build from the ground up.
Why do you focus so heavily on website speed and mobile optimisation?
The vast majority of local trade and service searches happen on smartphones, often by someone who wants an issue fixed fast. If your website takes more than a couple of seconds to load, or looks clunky on a mobile screen, prospects will tap out and call your competitor. We engineer every site to load instantly and function flawlessly on mobile.
Do you build landing pages for Google Ads or Meta Ads campaigns?
Yes. Sending paid traffic to your homepage is usually a mistake, since homepages have too many distractions. We build focused, single-purpose landing pages matched to exactly what someone searched for or clicked on, which is one of the fastest ways to lower your cost per lead.
How many pages does my Wollongong business actually need?
It depends entirely on what you offer and how you're found, but most Wollongong trade and service businesses land somewhere between 5 and 10 pages — home, about, individual service pages, a results or gallery page, and contact. More pages isn't automatically better; every extra page needs to earn its place.
Do you help with hosting and website maintenance?
Yes, completely. Secure, lightning-fast Australian hosting is built directly into our active web development plans so you never have to worry about surprise tech bills. Through thriiv Care & Maintenance, we also handle ongoing content changes, new photos, and security patches so your site always stays sharp and up to date.
Do you force me to move my website hosting to you?
Not at all. If you already have a reliable, fast hosting provider that you're happy with, we can build or optimise your website right where it is. We offer secure, high-speed hosting as a convenience, but the choice of where your digital assets live is entirely up to you.
Do you build websites for industries outside of trades?
Yes. Trades and home services make up a large part of who we work with, but we also build for professional services like accountants and solicitors, retail and hospitality businesses, and health and allied health practices. The principles — fast, trustworthy, built to convert — apply regardless of industry.
Do you only work with businesses in Wollongong?
Wollongong and the surrounding Illawarra is our core focus and where most of our clients are based, but we do work with businesses further afield where it makes sense. If you're outside the immediate area, get in touch and we'll be upfront about whether we're the right fit.
What if I've been burnt by a web design agency before?
More than a few of our clients have. You own your website and your domain from day one, full stop — nothing is held hostage, and if you ever decide to leave, you take everything with you. We'd rather earn your business every month than trap you into staying.
Can you help if I'm not sure what I actually need?
Yes — this is genuinely the most common starting point. Most business owners know something isn't working but aren't sure whether it's the website, the lack of visibility, or something else entirely. A quick, free website health check is usually the fastest way to find out.
Can you redesign my existing website without losing my Google rankings?
Yes, and it's one of the most important parts of any redesign. Before we touch anything, we audit what's currently ranking and where your traffic is coming from, then carefully map redirects from your old pages to the new ones during launch. Skipping this step is the most common way businesses lose their Google rankings overnight during a redesign — we don't let that happen.
Do I need to already have branding, logo, or content ready before we start?
Not necessarily. If you've already got branding and content, great — we'll work with what you have. If you don't, we can guide you through what's needed, help with copy, and source photography so you're not stuck trying to write your own website from scratch.
Will my new website actually rank on Google, or is that a separate service?
Your website is built to rank — clean structure, fast load times, local SEO signals from day one — but "built to rank" and "actively climbing the rankings every month" are two different things. The website gives you the foundation; ongoing local SEO is what pushes you from page two to the map pack over time. Plenty of clients start with just the website and add SEO later.
What happens after my website goes live — is that the end of the relationship?
Not at all — we treat launch as the start, not the finish. Every site we build comes with a care plan option so it stays fast, secure, and up to date, and we're on hand for content changes whenever you need something updated. Plenty of clients also come back later for SEO, ads, or lead automation once the site's live and doing its job.
