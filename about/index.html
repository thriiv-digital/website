Who We Are

Meet Ava — and the local team built to help Wollongong businesses thriiv.

Before thriiv, Ava spent several years inside a national digital agency, running point on over 600 client accounts as second-in-command.

She saw the pattern up close, over and over: genuinely good local businesses losing work online to competitors with worse products but sharper websites and better search visibility.

Moving back to Wollongong to close it properly — not with another distant account manager, but with a local team that actually knows the Illawarra market, shows up to the same networking rooms as its clients, and treats a dozen accounts with the attention a national agency gives its top one.

Founder Ava Webster
Based North Wollongong, NSW
Serving Wollongong & the Illawarra
Why "thriiv"

A website is supposed to make your business easier to find, easier to trust, and easier to hire.

Not a project you paid for once and then quietly stopped thinking about. Every build, every campaign, every SEO push is judged against one question: is this actually bringing in leads. If it isn't, it gets fixed, not defended.
